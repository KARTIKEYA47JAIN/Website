About Us

Your trusted partners
in business

Welcome to C tutor, an innovative platform
where learning meets opportunity, crafted with
passion by two visionary women from India.
Our story began with a simple yet profound
mission: to bridge the gap between dedicated
teachers and eager students.

At the heart of our vibrant startup is a dynamic
team that brings together diverse expertise to
drive innovation in educational technology. Led
by an experienced teacher well-versed in
multiple educational boards, our team
comprises talented software engineers, a
creative designer, and a savvy business
graduate. Guiding our journey are two
exceptional mentors: an industry veteran from
the prestigious IIT Kanpur and a seasoned
entrepreneur with three decades of multi-
industry experience. This powerful blend of
educational insight, technical prowess, design
thinking, and business acumen positions us to
revolutionize the way students learn and
teachers teach in the digital age.



Our Mission

To empower students in each corner of the world to make an
informed choice from a pool of quality tutors based on desired
location and mode of learning.

To empower teachers financially by providing them a
platform to reach out to a wider set of students online
and offline.

How We Help You As Students

C tutor is your gateway to finding the perfect teacher who
resonates with your learning style and academic needs. Our
platform allows you to search for teachers by subject, check their
availability, and secure your slot with a simple payment process. It is
education tailored for you, at your
convenience.

How We Help You As Teachers

For teachers, we provide a space to showcase your skills, connect with students, and grow your teaching practice. C tutor empowers
you to list your services, manage your schedules, and expand your
reach to students across the globe.
